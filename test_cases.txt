=Matrix Operation :

==Addition & Subtraction

1 2
3 4

5 6
7 8

6  8
10 12

-4 -4
-4 -4

matrix A (2*3):
1 2 3
4 5 6

matrix B (2*2):
1 2
3 4

=Vector Operation :

==Addition & Subtraction & Dot Product
Vector A: [1 2 3 4]
Vector B: [5 6 7 8]

A + B: [6 8 10 12]
A - B: [-4 -4 -4 -4]
Dot Product = 70

-Error Example-
Vector A: [1 2 3]
Vector B: [1 2 3 4]

Result: Error: Vector sizes must match for addition, subtraction & dot product

==Scalar
Vector A: [1 2 3]
Scalar: 2.5

2.5 * A: [2.5 5 7.5]

==Cross Product
Vector A: [1 2 3]
Vector B: [4 5 6]

Cross Product = [-3, 6, -3]

-Error Examples-
Vector A: [1 2]
Vector B: [3 4]

Result: Error: only 3D vectors.

Vector A: [1 2 3]
Vector B: [4 5]

Result: Error: only 3D vectors.

==Magnitude
Vector A: [3 4 0]

Magnitude = 5

==Normalization

magnitude = 5

Normalized = [0.6, 0.8, 0]

-Error Example-
Vector A: [0 0 0]
magnitude = 0

Result: Error: Cannot normalize zero vector.
